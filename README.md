# Verify Stamp

Verify Stamp is a secure document verification infrastructure designed for educational institutions, organizations, and credential-issuing bodies.

It allows institutions to issue, verify, and validate official documents without manual review, reducing fraud, administrative burden, and verification delays.

---

## What Problem Verify Stamp Solves

Educational institutions issue millions of documents every year, including:
- Transcripts
- Enrollment letters
- Certificates
- Verification forms

These documents are often:
- Manually verified
- Emailed insecurely
- Easily altered or forged
- Time-consuming to confirm

Verify Stamp replaces manual verification with a secure, automated validation layer.

---

## What Verify Stamp Does

- Provides a unique verification stamp for each document
- Allows third parties to confirm authenticity instantly
- Reduces document fraud and misuse
- Supports compliance and audit readiness
- Integrates without replacing existing systems

---

## Who Verify Stamp Is For

- Colleges and universities
- Training programs
- Credentialing organizations
- Employers verifying academic records
- Compliance-driven institutions
